Government Compliance

Ensuring regulatory adherence, national security, and data protection for government institutions.

Key Compliance Standards

  • FISMA: Federal Information Security Modernization Act â€“ Establishes cybersecurity controls for government agencies.
  • NIST 800-53: Comprehensive security and privacy guidelines for federal systems.
  • ISO 27001: Global standard for information security management.
  • GDPR: Regulates data protection for government entities handling EU citizen information.
  • HIPAA: Protects sensitive healthcare data within government health departments.

Best Practices

  • Implement a Zero Trust security framework for all government IT systems.
  • Enforce strong encryption and multi-factor authentication across government networks.
  • Conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Ensure compliance with local and international cybersecurity regulations.
  • Establish strict data access controls and continuous monitoring for threat detection.

Security Measures

  • Deploy endpoint detection and response (EDR) solutions for real-time threat mitigation.
  • Utilize AI-driven analytics for proactive cyber risk management.
  • Enforce security policies through automation and governance frameworks.
  • Maintain 24/7 network monitoring and incident response protocols.
