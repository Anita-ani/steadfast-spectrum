Retail & E-Commerce Compliance

Ensuring data security, fraud prevention, and regulatory compliance for online and offline retail.

Key Compliance Standards

PCI DSS: Protects cardholder data during transactions.

GDPR: Governs customer data handling in the EU.

CCPA: Ensures data privacy rights for California residents.

ISO 27001: Establishes an Information Security Management System (ISMS).

FTC Safeguards Rule: Requires businesses to protect consumer financial information.

Best Practices

Implement **SSL/TLS encryption** for secure online transactions.

Use **tokenization** to protect stored credit card details.

Require **multi-factor authentication (MFA)** for customer accounts.

Perform **regular penetration testing** for security gaps.

Leverage **AI-powered fraud detection** to prevent identity theft.

Security Measures

Restrict access to **customer payment data** with role-based controls.

Monitor for **card skimming attacks** on checkout pages.

Ensure compliance with **PCI DSS** for secure payment processing.

Secure API communication for **third-party integrations**.
