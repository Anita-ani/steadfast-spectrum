Healthcare Compliance

Ensuring patient data security, privacy, and regulatory compliance in healthcare.

Key Compliance Standards

HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)

Protects patient health information (PHI) in the U.S.

GDPR

Regulates patient data protection in the EU.

HITECH Act

Enhances HIPAA enforcement for digital healthcare data security.

ISO 27799

Provides guidance for information security management in healthcare

NIST Cybersecurity Framework: Establishes security controls for healthcare organizations.

NIST Cybersecurity Framework

Provides best practices for technology security.

Best Practices

  • Implement end-to-end encryption for patient records and healthcare databases.
  • Enforce multi-factor authentication (MFA) for healthcare professionals accessing data
  • Regularly conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.
  • Ensure compliance with international and regional data protection regulations.
  • Use AI-driven threat detection to monitor for security breaches.

Security Measures

  • Apply access control policies to restrict unauthorized PHI access.
  • Use blockchain for secure patient record management and data integrity.
  • Conduct regular HIPAA and GDPR compliance audits.
  • Enable real-time monitoring of medical IoT devices to prevent cyber threats.
